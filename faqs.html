FAQs

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about our products and services:

  • Where do you deliver to?

    We deliver to London and the surrounding areas. Please get in touch if you're unsure if your area is covered.

  • Is there a delivery charge?

    Yes, there is a delivery charge. The cost will depend on the distance; this can be discussed in the initial consultation.

  • How much do your cakes cost?

    All of our cakes are bespoke so it's challenging to offer an estimate. The price will depend on the number of tiers, the decoration and the size of the cake.

    We will discuss the price in your initial consultation and we will try our best to find a solution that works for you.

  • Do you make vegan or gluten free cakes?

    We're happy to make vegan cakes.

    We cannot provide true gluten free cakes as there are cross contamination issues. However, we can make cakes without direct addition of gluten.

  • What is the process for ordering a wedding cake?

    Our journey will start with an initial consultation where we can discuss your requirements and preferences.

    This will be followed by a tasting session where you can decide on which flavours you would like for each tier.

    We will have regular catch ups and be in contact throughout the process.

    We will deliver to the wedding venue and stack the cake on site.

  • Do you make wedding favours?

    Yes, we make a variety of bespoke wedding favours. Please get in contact with us to discuss your ideas.

  • What is the process for ordering celebration cake?

    Contact us for an email or telephone consultation where we can discuss your requirements and preferences.

    We will deliver the cake on the day.
